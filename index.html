About

With a dynamic energy to his work, his sound is responsible for creating musical journies at events, in theatres, and in clubs. With his father's love for the rhythm, Ipnoz grew up a keen DJ, playing to the sounds of the city; winning over every party he plays.

He works internationally, bringing the sound of his city to lives across the world. For him, it's about creating a sound that can become style it's own.

Doing this, he has influenced many clubs in France, many African Countries, The US, The Caribbean and has had a pivotal role in bringing Afrobeats to life in Curacao.

His artistical influence draws from artists like Snoop Dogg, Method Man & Redman, Bone Thugs' n' Harmony, Flo Rida, Miguel, Maitre Gims and Trackdilla and DJs' collaborations from Krisfader, Young Son, MystykalKut, Ewone, Alex Gewoon, Noise and more.

It's music that pairs perfectly to products from Belvedere, Luc Belaire and New Era to Coca Cola.

Immersed on the scene, Ipnoz has truly encaptured the sound of the island, keeping his ear to the ground as a curator of eclectic events.

With a clear vision for his future on the underground music scene, the city will never sound the same with Ipnoz around.
